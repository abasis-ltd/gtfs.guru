Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Data Processing

GTFS.Guru processes all data locally in your browser using WebAssembly technology. We do not upload your GTFS feeds to our servers. Your data remains on your device at all times.

We use anonymous usage statistics to improve the application. No personally identifiable information (PII) is collected.
